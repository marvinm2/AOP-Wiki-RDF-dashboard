About this page

Evolution and growth patterns of the AOP-Wiki database over time, using quarterly releases. Use the global Show Deltas toggle at the top to switch every plot between absolute counts and per-version deltas, or the per-plot checkbox. Use the Range selectors to constrain every snapshot-keyed plot to a sub-window; CSV/PNG/SVG downloads and the methodology "Run on Endpoint" queries honour the same window. Lifetime / creation-date plots are not affected by the range selector — their x-axis is creation year, not snapshot date.
